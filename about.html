Hi again, here's the section where you can find out all about me, my life, my expertise in web design and why I'm qualified for the job you need me to do! Apart from web design, I enjoy many other hobbies such as science, skiing, backpacking, going out with friends, and playing the guitar. I even have a song of my own, "Don't Call Me Home" out on Spotify
